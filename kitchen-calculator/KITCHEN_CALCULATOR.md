# Kitchen Calculator

Calculates conversions between US baking measurements and milliliters (mL).

| Unit to Convert    | Volume (in mL) |
|--------------------|---------------|
| mL                | 1             |
| US cup           | 240           |
| US fluid ounce   | 30            |
| US teaspoon      | 5             |
| US tablespoon    | 15            |


Explores the type data strcuture and pattern matching in named functions.
